About Ana

The Garden of My Heart

Hi, I'm Ana Zaffiro — and I'm here to walk this journey with you.

My work blends intuitive energy clearing with grounded, real-life support. I'm here for individuals, homes, and businesses who want more clarity, balance, and alignment — whether you're moving through emotional ups and downs, facing big decisions, or feeling that quiet pull to come back home to yourself.

We do this through what I call The Love Alignment Process. It's a gentle mix of energy work, heart-centered coaching, and channeled guidance, but nothing is ever forced or rushed. We simply meet what's ready, move at your pace, and let healing unfold in its own time.

You were never broken — you've just been carrying more than your heart was meant to hold on its own. My role isn't to fix you. It's to help you gently set some of that down, clear what's been weighing you down, and reconnect with the steady wisdom and energy that's always been there for you. When your heart feels safe to open again, you naturally start to feel more like yourself — clear, radiant, and at ease. My hope is to make energy healing feel normal and accessible for everyday people, so more of us can experience the peace and power that comes from living with an open, aligned heart.

"When we trust the heart, energy follows. When energy aligns, life begins to feel possible again — gentle, radiant, and beautifully your own."
